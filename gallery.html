Our Dogs In Care

Angel

Angel was born into Keeler K-9 services after her mom(Emily) and dad(Duke) were rescued, unbeknownst that Emily was already pregnant. A couple weeks went by and she had her litter, and we named her Angel due to the white, angel wing like spot on her chest. At that moment in time she was the exact Angel i needed.

Snow

Snow was an amazing dog with a huge personality. Unfortunately, she was overcome with cancer. But not without giving cancer a fight for its money. The vet gave her a couple weeks to live, but with non-traditional herbal remedies and RSO(Rick Simpson Oil), she pushed on and lived multiple months longer. She loved playing in the Snow, hence her name.

Jaxson

Jaxson was a very well behaved puppy. He loved being around children, family and receiving cuddles.

Duke

Duke, father of Angel, was a big ol cuddler. Dont let his size fuel ya, he behaved more like a lap dog.

Emily

Emily, mother of Angel was an amazing albeit stubborn dog. She would bump into everything when she walked around nothing was safe.

King Kong

Kong came to us in a time that we needed him most. He quickly bonded with his Sister Snow, and my Son. Kong was as big a dog as they come, standing almost 4 feet on all fours, but dont let his size fool you, King Kong would never harm anyone.

Bella

Bella was a black lab. She was loved very much by everyone she met always smiling at you.
