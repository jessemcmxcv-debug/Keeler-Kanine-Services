Our Mission

At Keeler K-9 Services, our mission is to protect the bond between people and their dogs during life’s most unexpected moments. When an owner is suddenly unable to provide care due to arrest, hospitalization, or emergency situations, we step in to ensure every dog remains safe, supported, and treated with dignity.

We provide secure temporary housing, responsible rehousing, and compassionate care through a trusted network of handlers, foster partners, and animal care professionals. Every placement is guided by the dog’s health, temperament, and emotional well-being.

Our commitment goes beyond shelter. We believe every dog deserves stability, structure, and respect regardless of circumstance. By offering transparency, professionalism, and empathy, Keeler K-9 Services exists to bring peace of mind to both owners and their companions when it matters most.
